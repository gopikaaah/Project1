AboutUs

OUR INSPIARTION Welcome to The Upside Down Market, a fan-made store inspired by the unforgettable world of Stranger Things. Born from a deep love for the show's 80s nostalgia, supernatural mystery, and unforgettable characters, our mission is to bring the magic of Hawkins, Indiana into everyday life. Whether you're a die-hard member of the Hellfire Club, a loyal friend like Dustin, or a brave soul like Eleven, our store offers something for every kind of fan. We’ve carefully curated a wide range of products that celebrate the spirit of the series—from retro-inspired apparel and accessories, to collectible figures, home decor, and replica props straight out of the show. Dive into the Upside Down with themed posters, cozy blankets, Dungeons & Dragons dice sets, and exclusive party supplies perfect for Stranger Things-themed events. Every item is chosen to help fans relive the most iconic moments and express their love for the series in style. At The Upside Down Market, we don’t just sell merchandise—we create a space for fans to connect with the show’s world, its themes of friendship, bravery, and adventure, and the quirky charm that made it a global phenomenon. Whether you're shopping for yourself or looking for the perfect gift, you're sure to find something strange, nostalgic, and totally awesome.

PRODUCT QUALITY At The Upside Down Market, quality is our top priority. Every product in our collection is carefully selected or custom-made to ensure it meets high standards of craftsmanship, durability, and design. From soft, comfortable fabrics in our apparel to detailed, officially licensed collectibles and well-crafted home decor, we make sure every item delivers both style and substance. Whether it’s a Hellfire Club hoodie, a replica prop, or a themed mug, you can trust that you're getting a product built to last—and made to be loved by true fans.
